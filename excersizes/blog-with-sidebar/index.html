City Of Ghosts

Edinburgh, Scotland

Part One : The Inspecters

In "City of Ghosts" by V.E. Schwab, the first book in the Cassidy Blake series, we meet our protagonist, Cassidy, who has a unique ability to see ghosts. Cassidy's parents are paranormal investigators who have a successful TV show, but Cassidy is the only one who can actually interact with spirits. When her parents are offered the opportunity to film a new episode in Edinburgh, Scotland, Cassidy is thrilled because Edinburgh is known for its haunted history.

Part Two : City Of Ghosts

In Edinburgh, Cassidy encounters the mysterious and alluring ghost, Jacob, who saved her life when she was younger and has been by her side ever since. She also discovers that she is a "Veil Walker," someone who can cross the veil between the living and the dead. This revelation comes at a crucial time, as Cassidy finds herself drawn into the world of the dead, facing dangerous spirits called "Red Hands" who can harm both the living and the dead.

Part Three : Ghost Hunters

As Cassidy navigates this new world, she must learn to control her abilities while also uncovering the truth about her own past and the mysterious circumstances surrounding her connection to the supernatural. Along the way, she forms a deep bond with Jacob and begins to understand the complexities of life, death, and the afterlife.

Part Four : The Raven In Red

The first book sets the stage for Cassidy's journey as she discovers her powers, confronts dangerous spirits, and unravels the secrets of her own identity. It's a captivating blend of mystery, adventure, and supernatural elements that keeps readers eagerly turning pages.

Bridge Of Souls

The French Quarter

Part One : Sugar And Skulls

"Bridge of Souls" is the third book in V.E. Schwab's Cassidy Blake series. In this installment, Cassidy and her ghostly best friend Jacob embark on a new adventure, this time to New Orleans, Louisiana, where Cassidy's parents are filming a new episode of their paranormal TV show.

Part Two : The Voice In The Dark

As Cassidy explores the vibrant city of New Orleans, she encounters a sinister presence lurking in the shadows. Mysterious disappearances and ghostly sightings lead Cassidy to uncover a dangerous network of malevolent spirits known as the "Veilbreakers," who seek to tear down the barrier between the living and the dead.

Part Three : The Society Of The Black Cat

With the help of her friends and allies, including Jacob and a fellow Veil Walker named Lara, Cassidy sets out to stop the Veilbreakers and protect both the living and the dead.

Part Four : The Emissary Of Death

Along the way, she learns more about her own powers and confronts the dark forces that threaten to unleash chaos upon the world. "Bridge of Souls" is a gripping tale of friendship, bravery, and the battle between light and darkness.

Part Five : The Bridge Of Souls

As Cassidy navigates the haunted streets of New Orleans, she must confront her greatest fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of both the living and the dead. With its blend of supernatural thrills and heartfelt emotion, "Bridge of Souls" is sure to captivate readers from beginning to end.
