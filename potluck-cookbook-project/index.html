Marry Me Chicken Tortellini

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 50 minutes Total Time: 1 hour Servings: 6 (serving size:1 1/3 cups)

Ingredients

  • 4 thick-cut bacon slices
  • 2 (8 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast (pounded to 1 inch thickness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons of kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 a cup of finely chopped shallot
  • 4 large garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried italian seasoning
  • 1/2 a teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 tablesppons tomato paste
  • 1 (20 ounce) package refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 1/2 a cup of pre grated parmesan cheese + more for garnish
  • Torn fresh basil leaves

Directions

1. Gather all ingredients; Fill a large saucepan with salted water and bring to a boil over high heat.

2. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat; Add chopped bacon; Cook, stirring occasionally, until rendered and crispy, about 8 minutes; Using a slotted spoon, transfer bacon to a paper towel-lined plate to drain, reserving about 3 tablespoons of drippings in skillet

3. While bacon cooks, sprinkle chicken evenly with pepper and 1/4 teaspoon of the salt; Add chicken to skillet with bacon drippings; Cook over medium-high, until chicken is browned and cooked through (a thermometer inserted into thickest portion of chicken registers 165 degrees F (73 degreesC)), 5 to 6 minutes per side, reducing heat if bottom of pan gets too dark. Transfer chicken to a plate; set aside until cool enough to handle, about 10 minutes. Chop chicken into bite-sized pieces.

4. Reduce heat to medium, add sundried tomatoes and shallot; Cook, stirring often, until softened, about 2 minutes; Add garlic, italian seasoning, crushed red pepper, and remaining 1 teaspoon of salt. Cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add tomato paste; cook, stirring constantly, until deepened in color, for about 1 minute.

5. Pour chicken broth in the mixture; cook, stirring and scraping constantly to deglaze the skillet, about 1 minute. Stir in cream and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened, 3 to 5 minutes.

6. Add tortellini to boiling water, and boil until tortellini are tender and float to the surface, 3 to 5 minutes. Reserve 1 cup of cooking water, then drain.

7. Stir parmesan cheese, reserved cooked bacon, reserved chopped chicken, and tortellini into skillet with cream mixture. Cook, stirring and tossing constantly, until evenly coated in sauce. Add reserved cooking water, 1/4 cup at a time, if necessary to maintain a creamy consistency. Divide mixture among 6 bowls; garnish with addditional parmesan cheese and a generous scattering of fresh basil.

Worlds Best Lasagna

Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 2 hours 30 minutes Total Time: 3 hours 15 minutes Servings: 12 Additional Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pounds of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic (crushed)
  • 1 (28 ounces) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounces) cans of canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounces) cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 a cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of fennle seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces of riccotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pounds of mozzerlla cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup of grated parmessan cheese

Directions

1. Gather all your ingredients.

2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.

3. Stir in crushed tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water; Season with sugar, 2 tbs of parsley, basil, 1 tsp of salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tbs of parsley, and 1/2 tsp of salt.

6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

7. To Assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles legthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzerella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over mozzerella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cups of parmesan cheese.

8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzerella and parmesan cheese, Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil wiht cooking spray or make sure th foil does not touch the cheese.

9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.

10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.

Homemade Mac and Cheese

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 45 minutes Total Time: 55 minutes Servings: 4

Ingredients

Macaroni and Cheese

  • 8 ounces of uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of salted butter
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups of milk, or more as needed
  • 2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of of finely grated parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste (optional)

Bread Crumb Topping

  • 2 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 1/2 cup of dry bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch of ground paprika

Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch square baking dish.

2. Make the macaroni and cheese: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and simmmer, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; it will finish cooking in the oven. Drain and transfer to the prepared baking dish.

3. While the macaroni is cooking, melt 1/4 cup of butter in a medium skillet over low heat. Whisk in flour and stir until mixture becomes paste-like and light golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.

4. Gradually whisk 2 1/2 cups of milk into the flour mixture, and bring to a simmer. Stir in shredded cheddar and finely grated parmesan cheese; season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce has thickened, 3 to 5 minutes, adding up to 1/2 cup more of milk if needed. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni and stir until well combined.

5. Make the bread crumb topping: Melt 2 tbs of butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add bread crumbs; cook and stir until well coated and browned. Spread bread crumbs over macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.

6. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is golden brown and macaroni and cheese is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
